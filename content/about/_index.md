I'm Michael Rutter and this is my half-baked attempt at blogging as though it's
still 2008. I work as a software engineer in London-ish UK. I am a self-taught
programmer and professional philosophy PhD dropout.

Topics that I am interested in include programming, philosophy, politics, video
games, board games, cardboard, and coffee.

I enjoy exploring different programming languages and picking up new ones,
particularly those with interesting type systems like TypeScript, Rust, and
Haskell. Philosophy-wise, I was mostly interested in metaphysics and causation. There is a surprising amount of crossover between philosophy and programming - the lineage from Frege through Russell, Church, and eventually getting to modern type theory, which probably explains a lot.
